Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for multiple jobs?

We want applicants to think deeply about their skills and interest—and how they line up with the jobs available at Riot.

It's not a good sign if you find yourself applying to as many open positions as possible.

I interviewed/applied last year and wasn’t selected. Can I reapply?

Needs change, and people grow. If you've leveled up since your last application, feel free to apply to the appropriate role.​
